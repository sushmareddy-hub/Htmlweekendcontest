The Beagle Blog

The modern beagle hounds that we see was developed around the 1830s in Great Britain. Beagles are possibly the result of crosses between the Harrier, Talbot Hound, North County Beagle and Southern Hound. The beagle breed was specifically created to track and hunt rabbits and hares by their scent.Many of us make the mistake of clubbing beagles with other small breeds like Shih Tzu, Lhasa apso, Pomeranian and pugs. However, despite their superbly cute looks, beagles are in fact scenthounds.
